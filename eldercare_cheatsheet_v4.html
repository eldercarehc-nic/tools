Driving & Special Policies
Driving: EC does not recommend aides drive clients. If requested → driving waiver + $5/hr driving rate + client provides vehicle and insurance.
Cash / valuables: EC strongly advises against. If client chooses to share → EC bears no liability for loss, misuse, or unauthorized transactions.
Hazard / special circumstances: EC may adjust service level during medical emergencies, severe weather, or national emergencies. Hazard Pay may apply.
