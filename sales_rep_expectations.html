Visit Planner
Visit Planner tracking must be completed and submitted to your manager before your 1-on-1. Any changes to the plan must be noted.
Account Visit Tiers
● High Value Accounts
Minimum once per month — in-person visit required. These are your priority accounts and must be maintained consistently.
● All Other Accounts
Must be regularly visited in person. Consistent coverage shows active management and protects your territory.
▲ At Risk
Accounts that are not regularly visited are at risk of reassignment. Do not let accounts go cold.

WellSky Expectations
After a Case Starts
Once a case starts, verify that you are correctly assigned as the marketer on the case in WellSky. Do not assume it was set automatically.
Marketer assignmentVerify after every case start
Referral Source Missing Report
Every Friday you will receive a Referral Source Missing report. If your name appears on the report, your referral sources must be updated in WellSky.
All missing referral sources from the current month must be resolved by end of month — no exceptions.
How to Resolve
Option A: Update the referral sources yourself directly in WellSky
Option B: Send the relevant information to the Sales Administrator to update on your behalf
