The Email Thread Rule
All acknowledgements live in the original lead email thread. Do not create new emails or communicate outside the thread for any step in this process.
Acknowledge receipt → reply in thread within 15 min
Confirm outreach → reply in thread after calling
Final outcome → reply in thread when lead is closed
Lead Completion Checklist
Use this before marking any lead as done.
  • Replied in thread — acknowledged receipt within 15 min
  • Placed first call within 30 minutes
  • Replied in thread — confirmed outreach was made
  • If no answer — followed up min. 3 times
  • All follow-ups done within 5 business days
  • Replied in thread — final outcome logged
  • Lead added to HubSpot
Non-Negotiables
Speed is everything. A lead not acknowledged or called within the window is gone — reassigned to the next rep, no exceptions.
Close every loop in the thread. Every lead — won, lost, or unreachable — must be replied to in the original email thread and logged in HubSpot.
