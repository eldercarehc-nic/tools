Split days don't count as shadow days. The full day must be dedicated.

Weeks 2–3+ Shadow Phase — 9 Required Visits Each visit must be a full dedicated day ›
≥48 hrs before
Pre-shadow email to rep/ops director — Dir. Sales Ops
Purpose of visit, key topics, expectations, referral-asking component. Required — not optional.
Night before
Reminder to new hire — Sales Admin
Rep name, phone, full address with suite, parking/access notes.
By 6 PM
New hire submits Google Form debrief
3 prompts: (1) What I observed (2) One unanswered question (3) One thing I'll do differently. Visit does not count until submitted.

Shadow rep sends 3-sentence debrief to Dir. Sales Ops
(1) What we covered (2) One thing done well (3) One area to develop. All three items required for the visit to count.
